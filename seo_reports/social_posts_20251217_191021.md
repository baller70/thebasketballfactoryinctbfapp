## Analysis & Recommendations

### Why Duplicate Content Was Detected
Twitter/X has strict duplicate content policies to prevent spam. The current script uses:
- Limited template variations (4 templates)
- Same program URLs repeatedly
- Similar hashtag combinations
- Random selection that may repeat recent posts

### Recommended Improvements

#### 1. **Enhanced Content Variation**
- Expand template library from 4 to 20+ unique variations
- Include dynamic elements: dates, stats, testimonials, questions
- Rotate through different program features and benefits
- Add seasonal or timely references

#### 2. **Smart Duplicate Prevention**
- Track recently posted content in database
- Implement cooldown period (24-48 hours) before reposting similar content
- Check last N posts before generating new content
- Use content fingerprinting to detect similarity

#### 3. **Content Diversification**
- Mix promotional posts with educational content
- Share basketball tips, training advice, success stories
- Include coach spotlights, player achievements, facility updates
- Vary hashtag combinations more significantly

#### 4. **Timing Optimization**
- Space posts at least 4-6 hours apart
- Align posting times with peak engagement hours
- Avoid posting identical content on same day
- Implement daily content calendar with unique themes

#### 5. **URL Parameter Variation**
- Add UTM parameters with timestamps: `?utm_source=twitter&utm_campaign=daily_post_20251217_1`
- Use different landing pages for similar programs
- Rotate between program overview, registration, and details pages
