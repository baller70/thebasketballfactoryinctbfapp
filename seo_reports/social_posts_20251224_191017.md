## 🎯 Why Duplicate Content Detection?

Twitter's duplicate content detection prevents:
1. **Spam:** Repeated identical posts
2. **Bot Behavior:** Automated posting of same content
3. **User Experience:** Cluttered timelines with duplicate tweets

### How the Script Handles This
The script generates posts based on:
- Top-performing content (changes weekly)
- Recent program updates (changes when programs are updated)
- Random template selection for variety

However, if:
- The same programs remain top-performing
- No new programs are added
- Templates generate similar content

Then duplicate detection may trigger, which is **normal and expected**.
